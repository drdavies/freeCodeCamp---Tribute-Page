The life of the legendary American singer

  • 1932 - Born J.R Cash in Kingsland, Arkansas, USA.
  • 1950 - Elnisted in the Air Force and sent to the U.S. Air Force Security Service at Landsberg, West Germany.
  • 1954 - Honorably discharged as a staff sergeant, moved to Memphis, Tennessee with his first wife Vivian.
  • 1955 - Made his first recordings at Sun Records, "Hey Porter" and "Cry! Cry! Cry!".
  • 1962 - Started touring with the Carter Family.
  • 1968 - Married his second wife, June Carter.
  • 1969 - Starred in his own TV show, The Johnny Cash Show.
  • 1972 - Met with President Richard Nixon to discuss prison reform.
  • 1985 - Formed the country supergroup The Highwaymen with Waylon Jennings, Willie Nelson, and Kris Kristofferson.
  • 2003 - Died in Nashville, Tennessee aged 71.
